Shounen

Shounen anime are any Japanese animated shows that are likely to appeal to a young-teen male audience. The term shounen (also romanized as ‘shonen’ or ‘shōnen’) roughly means ‘boy’ or ‘youth’. Shounen was first used for characterising manga, from which many anime are adapted.

Shoujo

Shoujo anime is a term that refers to anime and manga that are designed to appeal to a young female audience.Shoujo means young woman in Japanese, and it is both an editorial and a demographic category.Shoujo anime typically features themes such as first love, friendship, growing up, and slice of life.Shoujo anime often has beautiful characters and a harem of handsome guys for the main female character.

Kodomuke

Literally, the word kodomomuke translates from Japanese into English as "intended for children." You may have guessed it, but the term is used to described an anime which is specifically geared towards young children who are typically under the age of 13

Naruto Shippuden

Naruto: Shippuden is an anime television series mainly adapted from Part II of Masashi Kishimoto 's original Naruto manga series, with exactly 500 episodes. It is set two and a half years after the original series in the Naruto universe, following the teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki and his allies.

Black Clover

Black Clover is an anime series that follows the adventures of Asta,a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything.He receives a rare grimoire that grants him anti-magic abilities,and joins the Black Bulls,a squad of misfit mages.Along with his childhood friend Yuno,who is gifted with exceptional magical powers,Asta aims to become the next Wizard King,the leader of the Clover Kingdom.

Mashle

Mashle: Magic and Muscles is an anime set in a magical world where one’s magical abilities are easily identified by a distinctive mark on their face. However, Mash Burnedead, an aloof boy with superhuman strength but no magical abilities, lives a quiet life with his father, far removed from society.

One Punch Man

One-Punch Man is a Japanese anime series based on the webcomic created by One and its subsequent manga adaptation illustrated by Yusuke Murata.The story is set in City Z and follows Saitama,a superhero who has grown bored as he has become so powerful that all of his battles end in a single punch.Saitama is an exceptionally powerful hero who easily defeats the monsters or other villains with a single punch.

Fairytale

Fairy Tail is a captivating Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hiro Mashima.The story unfolds in the magical realm of Earth-land, where wizards and fantastical creatures coexist.

Bleach

Bleach is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo.The series follows Ichigo Kurosaki,a high school student who can see spirits and becomes a Soul Reaper.Soul Reapers are death personifications who fight against Hollows,corrupted souls who prey on humans and other spirits.The series also explores the Soul Society,the afterlife realm of the Soul Reapers.
